Costco

Costco.com

Costco is a very well known store that has amazing discounts. They use this and the graphic design element of Visual Heiarchy to their advantage as your eye is first drawn to the large and interesting words on their page, which are the biggest current deals.

Repetition

Nothing Bundt Cakes

nothingbundtcakes.com

For this small bakery shop, using repitition and a cute design make the whole experience feel more homey. There is reppetition in the colors and stitching pattern around the site, as well as fonts and format.

Contrast

Converse

Converse.com

This striking page really draws in the eye with the use of contrast. The way that the ads pop really helps create interest and that is thanks to their very effective use of contrast in their designs.
