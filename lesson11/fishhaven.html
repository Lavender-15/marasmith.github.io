Fishing spots in Fishhaven!

For many years, Fish Haven has been known for its unique and incredible fishing spots. As an origional fishing town we have become a destination for those seeking to catch some trout! with numerous lakes and rivers around, including places such as Bear Lake Stat Park, and Hyrum State Park. Fishing here is a huge part of our culture, and has brought in new folks since the days of the wild west. Make sure to check out all of our fishing spots, so you can hit every one on the list!
