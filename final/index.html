Our Goals

We are the Boise Idaho Chamber of Commerce, and we are here to serve you in the Boise region. We strive to be an advocate for finding a vibrant and outstanding quality of life, with a more prosperous economy. Our suport focuses on four core areas: vitality, advocacy, member education, and member engagement. We want to be able to serve you, along with the thousands of businesses and employees we have been able to assist over the years.

Local Opportunities

Adventures In Boise

Adventures in Boise is a subscription mailing (think Wine of the Month) but instead of sending stuff, we send experiences. Every month, subscribers receive a fun, adventure-themed dossier with a ticket to go do something exciting at a local business: one month you might be swinging from a trapeze, while the next finds you playing a state-of-the-art virtual reality game. It could be almost anything! We can't wait for you to become a part of our adventuring family!
