Adventures In Boise

Adventures in Boise is a subscription mailing (think Wine of the Month) but instead of sending stuff, we send experiences. Every month, subscribers receive a fun, adventure-themed dossier with a ticket to go do something exciting at a local business: one month you might be swinging from a trapeze, while the next finds you playing a state-of-the-art virtual reality game. It could be almost anything!
