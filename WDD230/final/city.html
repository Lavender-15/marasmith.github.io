WELCOME TO BOISE!

In the state known for its potatoes, Boise has quite a lot more diversity than a lot of peple realize. Boise is known as a cultural center here in the Treasure Vally. With places like The Greenbelt, and the Art History Musem, and the old penitentery, there is a lot of history packed into this small area. Whether you are a resident, here for business, or just visiting, we welcome you to the City of Trees.

History In Boise

Here in boise, we have seen our share of history, starting by being a meeting place for several Native American Tribes, and becomming a full travel-town with the creation of the Owyhee (Silver City) mining areas (which were booming in the 1860s). With the combination of the mining and the travelers from the Oregon trail, Idaho city was the largest in the mid-west, paving the way for Boise to soon take its crown.
